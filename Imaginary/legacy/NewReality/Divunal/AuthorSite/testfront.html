What is Divunal, Anyway?

Divunal is a text based multiplayer game made with the Twisted Reality engine. It unlike most MUDs in that it praises roleplaying and problem solving over violence, and it is unlike most MOOs in that it has a structured, consistent world, with balanced rules and statistics, much like a tabletop roleplaying game. However, unlike any traditional RPG, the rule system is completely transparent to the players, and you'll never encounter a level, statistic, or percentage while playing your character. In short, it combines the best parts of all three of those types of games, and avoids most of their limitations.

In addition to it's engine and design, Divunal is a huge, constantly expanding world, with an extremely interactive environment and an unusual cast of characters. As your character, you will find yourself lost in a ruined city of amnesiacs, attempting to rediscover the complex history of your world and solve it's mysteries. In what has been compared to a shoving match between Roger Zelazny and Manga-style Anime, you will uncover ancient knowledge, meddle in the affairs of gods, and toy with a power that threatens to destroy what's left of your world in a unique, genre-transcending plot whose conclusion will be based on your actions.

If any of this sounds interesting, feel free to subscribe to our Divunal Mailing List for more information on the game and how to become a playtester. Our formal Divunal page will be up soon, so come back and visit often.
