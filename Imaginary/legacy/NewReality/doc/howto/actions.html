Build Your Own Action

Twisted Reality is a large and complex framework, but it's really designed to make it simple to express one simple idea: people do stuff, with things. In order to allow this, there are three different kinds of things that TR will talk about:

  • Things: Objects that exist in the world.
  • Actors: People who act on Things. (an Actor is a kind of Thing)
  • Actions: Things that Actors can do.

It is pretty uncommon to define entirely new kinds of objects or people, so this introductory tutorial focuses primarily on adding new kinds of Actions and the things that support them.
