Welcome to the Half-Assed Divunal Author Page!
Hello, and welcome to the Divunal Authoring Page. As its descriptive name implies, this page was thrown up as quickly as possible, and isn't even close to complete yet. You can expect it to be lacking in a number of areas, and still include a few mistakes, but try not to take it personally... If you have any questions, comments, or clarifications, feel free to email me or post open discussion questions to the Divunal Author mailing list, whichever seems more appropriate.

Also, check out the Author Contact List... Some of our other Authors are probably online right now, just waiting to answer your questions... Okay, so maybe they have other stuff to do, but you never know.   ;-)

Introduction

 

How to be an Author

A quick introduction to the world of Divunal, and helpful suggestions regarding the design, themes, and overall feel of the game.

What is Divunal?

An explaination of Divunal, and some of the ideas behind it.

How to Write Stuff

An introduction to simple object creation in divunal... Making people, places and things.

Divine Tutorial

A step by step tutorial of all the basic authoring techniques: Become a God in 7 days or less.

Coding for Divunal :
Make Your Own Verb

A potentially confusing but noble attempt to introduce new authors to the joy of writing Java verb code for Divunal. This may later divide into other separate categories as it improves, but currently it is a short introduction and overview followed by a brief example.

Reference

 

Plot Index & Timeline

The notable events leading up to and including the game we call Divunal. This section will hopefully also include links to other relevant pages for further information on the topics it lists.

People & Places :
The Encyclopaedia Divunalia

An index of the important people, places, and things in Divunal, with short summaries and links to other relevant pages for more information.

TR/Divunal Verb Documentation

A comprehensive list of the existing TR and Divunal verbs, their use, and their arguements, complete with examples for the non-computer-geek types in the audience.

Players & Statistics

Information on player characters, their statistics and abilities, and the mechanisms behind them. This also includes some coverage of the random player character generation features Divunal now includes.

Common Divunal Class_es

A detailed list of the Thing classes in Divunal, what they are, what they do, and why and how to use them to your advantage.
