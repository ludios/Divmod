To install the ClickChronicle firefox extension, click here. If you haven't previously downloaded the extension, a dialog may appear at the top of your browser's viewport indicating that the download was prevented for security reasons. To remedy this, complete the following steps:

  1. Click the "Edit Options" button on the right of the dialog
  2. Check that "clickchronicle.com" appears in the "Address of website" text box
  3. Click "Allow"
  4. Click "OK"
  5. Click here to reinstall the extension
